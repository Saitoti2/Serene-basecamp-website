Our Services

Experience the Best of Maasai Mara with Serene Basecamp

What We Offer

At Serene Basecamp Mara, we are committed to making your safari experience truly exceptional. We offer a range of services designed to immerse you in the natural beauty and wildlife of Maasai Mara. Our services include:

  • Game Drives

    Embark on thrilling game drives led by our expert guides to witness the incredible diversity of wildlife in the Maasai Mara.

  • Nature Walks

    Explore the wilderness on foot with guided nature walks, gaining a deeper understanding of the flora and fauna of the reserve.

  • Cultural Visits

    Connect with the local Maasai communities through cultural visits, where you can learn about their traditions and way of life.

  • Hot Air Balloon Safaris

    Soar above the plains on a hot air balloon safari, enjoying breathtaking aerial views of the wildlife below.

Pricing

Our services are tailored to meet the needs of different guests. Below are some of our pricing options:

  • Standard Package

    Starting from $120 per night, this package includes accommodation, daily game drives, and full board meals.

  • Luxury Package

    Starting from $360 per night, the luxury package offers additional amenities, such as spa treatments and private guided tours.

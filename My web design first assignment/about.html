About Us

Serene Basecamp Mara is a premier tourist lodge located in the heart of the Maasai Mara Game Reserve, in Narok County, Kenya. Our mission is to provide an unforgettable safari experience, blending the marvels of African wildlife with the warmth of Kenyan hospitality.

Founded in 2012, Serene Basecamp Mara has a rich history of offering guests an intimate connection with nature. We are committed to preserving the unique ecosystem of the Maasai Mara while providing top-notch accommodations and services to our visitors.

Our passionate and experienced team is dedicated to ensuring that every moment of your stay is a memorable one. From the thrill of game drives to the tranquility of our lodge, we offer an extraordinary escape into the wild.
